CCH1 Records with Coordinates

These are maps of CCH1 records that have coordinates (think of it as an autogeograph). In the map at the right the pixels are colored according to the number of collections at an approximate location, giving a visual representation of collecting effort.

Note that this represents well over two thirds of the California specimens in the CCH herbaria: 1778243 out of 2320817.
