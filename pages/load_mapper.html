Georeference Verification Mapping Tool

For submitting sets of coordinates to BerkeleyMapper

This tool is useful for verifying that county records are correctly georeferenced (turn on US County Boundaries under the Layers tab in BerkeleyMapper).

To start, paste latitude and longitude into the form below using the following format:

38.3452, -122.456
38.556, -122.456

(Separate latitude and longitude with a comma and one or more spaces. Use a minus sign for west longitude.)

or this format (comma delimited with quotes):

"UC1795736","37.5405","-121.5733","NAD 27","reference source","435","m"
or this (comma delimited format): 
GLAFUSC,0,sew00028,-18.20755576,145.7618865,WGS84
